Data Storage Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Trainer Launch is a product of Thryve Growth ("we", "us", "our"). This Data Storage Policy explains how customer information is stored and protected within the Trainer Launch platform.

Overview

Trainer Launch stores customer data only as necessary to provide platform functionality, maintain account services, support connected integrations, and comply with legal obligations.

We implement technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect customer information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Types of data stored

Depending on how you use Trainer Launch, we may store:

  • Account information such as name and email address
  • Business and marketing information submitted through onboarding and campaign setup
  • Uploaded content including images, videos, branding assets, and creative materials
  • Subscription and billing information provided through payment processors
  • Platform usage and analytics information
  • Connected platform metadata necessary to support integrations

Storage security

Customer data is protected using industry-standard security practices including:

  • Encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest where applicable
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • System monitoring and logging
  • Restricted access to production systems

Connected platform credentials

When customers connect third-party services such as Meta, Google Ads, Klaviyo, Duda, or Canva, access credentials and tokens are stored using encrypted storage mechanisms.

These credentials are used solely to perform actions explicitly authorized by the customer and are never sold or shared with third parties.

AI processing

Trainer Launch uses Anthropic Claude AI services to generate marketing assets, campaign recommendations, and content.

Customer content submitted for processing is used only to deliver requested functionality. Customer data and content are not used by Trainer Launch to train AI models.

Data location

Customer data may be processed and stored using secure cloud infrastructure providers located in the United States and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

By using Trainer Launch, you acknowledge that data may be transferred and processed across international borders where permitted by applicable law.

Access controls

Access to customer information is limited to authorized personnel, systems, and service providers that require access to operate, maintain, secure, or support the platform.

All access is governed by internal security procedures and contractual confidentiality obligations where applicable.

Data retention and deletion

Data storage duration is governed by our Data Retention Policy and Data Deletion Policy.

When retention periods expire or valid deletion requests are received, customer data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Data Storage Policy from time to time. Material changes may be communicated through email or in-app notifications.
