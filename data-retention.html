Data Retention Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Trainer Launch is a product of Thryve Growth ("we", "us", "our"). This Data Retention Policy explains how long we retain customer information and when that information is deleted.

Overview

We retain customer data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and enforce our agreements.

We do not sell customer data and we do not use customer content to train AI models.

Account data

Account information, including names, email addresses, subscription information, and platform settings, is retained while your account remains active.

When an account is deleted, personal information is scheduled for deletion and removed within 30 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Connected platform data

Trainer Launch may connect to third-party services including Meta, Google Ads, Klaviyo, and Duda.

Access tokens and connection credentials associated with these services are revoked and deleted within 30 days of account deletion or disconnection.

Marketing assets and uploaded content

Content uploaded by customers, including images, videos, branding assets, and campaign information, is retained while the account remains active.

Upon account deletion, this content is permanently deleted within 30 days unless retention is required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes.

Billing and audit records

Certain billing, transaction, and audit records may be retained for up to 90 days following account closure to support financial reconciliation, fraud prevention, security investigations, and legal compliance.

After the applicable retention period expires, the information is permanently deleted or anonymized.

Deletion requests

Customers may request deletion of their personal information at any time.

Deletion requests can be submitted through our Data Deletion page or by contacting us directly.

Most deletion requests are completed within 30 days of verification.

Data security

During any retention period, customer data remains protected using industry-standard security controls, including encryption, access controls, and monitoring.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Data Retention Policy from time to time. Material changes may be communicated through email or an in-app notification.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@trainerlaunch.app.
